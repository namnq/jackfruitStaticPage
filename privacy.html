Privacy Policy for Backup Theme App

This privacy policy explains how Backup Theme App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our app. Please read this policy carefully before using our app or submitting any personal information to us.

What personal information do we collect?

We collect the following personal information from you when you use our app:

  • Your name and email address, when you create an account or contact us.
  • Your device ID, model, operating system, and IP address, when you use our app or access our servers.
  • Your location, when you enable the location service on your device or app settings.
  • Your photos, contacts, and other data that you choose to backup using our app.
  • Your preferences, settings, feedback, and usage data, when you interact with our app or our services.

How do we collect your personal information?

We collect your personal information through various methods, such as:

  • When you provide it to us directly, such as when you create an account, contact us, or backup your data using our app.
  • When you use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies, such as when you access our app or our servers.
  • When you use third-party services or platforms, such as when you sign in with your social media account, or when you use our app on a device that has other apps installed.
  • When you use our SDKs, APIs, or other tools, such as when you integrate our app with other apps or services.

Why do we collect your personal information?

We collect your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the backup service, such as to store, process, and restore your data using our app or our servers.
  • To improve our app functionality, such as to fix bugs, optimize performance, or add new features.
  • To communicate with you, such as to send you notifications, updates, newsletters, or marketing messages.
  • To analyze and understand our users, such as to conduct research, surveys, or statistics.
  • To comply with our legal obligations, such as to respond to requests from authorities, courts, or regulators.
  • To protect our rights and interests, such as to enforce our terms and conditions, prevent fraud, or defend against claims.

How do we use your personal information?

We use your personal information in accordance with the purposes for which we collected it, and in compliance with the applicable privacy laws and regulations. We do not use your personal information for any other purposes without your consent, unless required or permitted by law.

How do we share your personal information?

We may share your personal information with the following parties, for the purposes described above:

  • Our affiliates, partners, or subsidiaries, who help us provide, improve, or promote our app or our services.
  • Our service providers, contractors, or vendors, who perform functions on our behalf, such as hosting, storage, analytics, payment, or marketing.
  • Our advertisers, sponsors, or third-party networks, who display ads or offer products or services that may be of interest to you, based on your preferences or usage data.
  • Our users, customers, or clients, who use our app or our services, such as when you backup or restore your data using our app or our servers.

  • Other parties, as required or permitted by law, such as when we respond to legal requests, comply with regulations, or protect our rights and interests.

We may also share aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data with third parties, which does not identify you or any other individual.

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties, unless you give us your consent, or as required or permitted by law. How do we protect your personal information?

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, or as required or permitted by law. When we no longer need your personal information, we delete it from our systems and servers, or anonymize it so that it cannot be linked back to you.
