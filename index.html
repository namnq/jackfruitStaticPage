About Us

We are a team of Shopify experts and enthusiasts who love creating and customizing online stores. We have been working with Shopify for over 5 years, and we have helped hundreds of merchants to launch and grow their businesses.

We know how important it is to have a beautiful and functional Shopify theme that reflects your brand and vision. We also know how frustrating it can be to lose your theme settings or files due to accidental changes, updates, or errors.

That's why we created Theme Backup, a Shopify app that helps you to easily backup, restore, and duplicate your Shopify theme. We wanted to give you a simple and reliable way to protect your online store's design and functionality.
